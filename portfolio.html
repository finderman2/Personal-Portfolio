Robert Cronin

Artist

Robert Cronin is one of the Berkshires better kept secrets, having shown internationally during the 70's and 80's, as well as having several exhibitions at prominent galleries, as well as being in the private collections of many famous people. Robert came to me asking for something that was very minimal and refined, so that the artwork could take center stage, which ranged from sculpture and figurative, to more recently a modernist “enclosure series”. The end result couldn’t have been a better fit, with the ability to easily add and edit content, such as an ever expanding and impressive portfolio and résumé.
